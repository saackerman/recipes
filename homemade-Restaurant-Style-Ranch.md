Homemade Restaurant-Style Ranch Recipe s s
Ingredients (5 total):

1 ranch seasoning packet (dump entire contents into bowl) s
1 cup buttermilk s
~1 tablespoon sour cream (about a 1-second squeeze from tub) s
1 cup mayonnaise with lime juice (or regular mayo + squeeze of lime juice) s
Sprinkle of fresh dill (or dill weed) s
Instructions
Combine all ingredients in a bowl:

Add ranch seasoning packet contents. s
Pour in 1 cup buttermilk. s
Add ~1 tbsp sour cream. s
Add 1 cup lime mayo. s
Sprinkle in dill. s
Mix thoroughly until well combined. s

Refrigerate to thicken slightly. s

Notes: Makes about a week's worth. Tastes like restaurant-style ranch. s s
