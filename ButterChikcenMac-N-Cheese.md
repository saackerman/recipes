# Butter Chicken Mac-N-Cheese

A fusion recipe combining the rich flavors of butter chicken with creamy mac and cheese.

## Table of Contents

  - [Spice Blend](#spice-blend)
- [Cooking Instructions](#cooking-instructions)

## Ingredients

- 900g (32oz) chicken breast, diced
- 2 onions, sautéed until browned
- 30g (2 Tbsp) tomato paste
- 30g (2 Tbsp) garlic paste
- 15g (1 Tbsp) ginger paste
- 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
- 400g (14oz) blended fire-roasted tomatoes
- 15g (1 Tbsp) sugar or zero-cal sweetener

### Spice Blend

- 2 Tbsp paprika
- 1 Tbsp chili powder
- 1 Tbsp spicy curry powder
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 Tbsp salt
- Dash of black pepper

## Cooking Instructions

1. Combine the diced chicken, sautéed onions, tomato paste, garlic paste, ginger paste, butter, blended fire-roasted tomatoes, sugar, and all spices in a slow cooker.

2. Cook on your preferred setting:
   - **High:** 2-3 hours
   - **Low:** 3-4 hours

## Final Preparation

1. Cook 672g shell pasta to 50% of the time indicated on the box. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

2. Add the following to the slow cooker with the cooked chicken mixture:
   - Partially cooked pasta
   - 1 cup pasta water
   - 400g plain 0% Greek yogurt (FAGE brand)
   - 120g 1/3 fat cream cheese
   - 227g extra sharp cheddar
   - 80g reduced fat mozzarella
   - 1 tsp garam masala

3. Stir well to combine all ingredients until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.

4. Garnish with chopped cilantro and green onions before serving.
